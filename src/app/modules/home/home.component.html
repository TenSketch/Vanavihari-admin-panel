Jungle Star, Valamuru

Jungle Star Eco Camp presents a distinctive island-like setting, featuring seven cottages perched on a hillock and accessed solely by a bridge. Administered by the Andhra Pradesh Forest Department, it offers an eco-friendly adventure with thrilling options such as treks and campfires. Nestled deep within the uncharted Eastern Ghats, Jungle Star camping site promises a one-of-a-kind overnight experience amidst nature.
